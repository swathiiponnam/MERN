Laver Cup is an international indoor hard court men's team tennis tournment

Laver cup has 2 teams, Team Europe and Team World. It was introduced in 2017 by the Rod Laver. Each team has a Coach, who is a legend of the sport and retired a long back.

Team Europe: In the first edition it has made Roger Federer as a captain of team Europe. And the star player Rafael Nadal is part of the team.

Team World: In the first edition of Laver Cup, They have entertaining players like Nike Kgyrious, Denis Shapalova, Alex Deminar.
